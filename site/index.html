Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Built for people who want to understand their spending without giving up their data.

Multi-Bank Support

HDFC, ICICI, Axis, Federal, Indian Bank, and more. Drop your PDF statements and go.

Auto-Import

Set a watch folder and statements are imported automatically. Or drag and drop.

Smart Categories

Auto-categorize by merchant with customizable rules. Create your own categories and keywords.

Rich Analytics

Spend trends, category breakdowns, top merchants, credit utilization. Filter across any dimension.

Transaction Tags

Define custom tags and apply them to transactions for granular tracking and filtering.

Multi-Card Views

Per-statement, cross-card consolidation, or fully custom filter combos. Your spend, your way.

Privacy

Your data stays yours. Period.

No cloud sync, no remote servers, no telemetry. Burnrate is a local-first application by design.

100% Local Storage

All data lives in a SQLite database on your device. Nothing is uploaded anywhere.

Zero Network Requests

The application never contacts external servers. Not even for updates.

No Telemetry

No analytics, no crash reports, no usage tracking. We don't know you exist.

Open Source

Every line of code is auditable. Trust through transparency, not promises.
